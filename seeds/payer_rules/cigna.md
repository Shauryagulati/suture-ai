# Cigna (commercial)

> **SIMULATED policy reference.** Derived from patterns in public Cigna
> coverage policies. Not authoritative.

Cigna's commercial coverage policies tend to be the strictest of the
five payers tracked here for advanced cardiac imaging — the plan
applies tighter appropriate-use criteria and is more aggressive on
post-payment recoveries. EviCore handles most PA decisions on Cigna's
behalf for cardiology.

## Prior-authorization posture

Cigna requires PA for LHC and EP study. For nuclear stress imaging,
Cigna applies PA more aggressively than other payers. EviCore's portal
is the primary submission channel, and the checklist UI is strict —
missing one item triggers a same-day pend rather than approval-with-
followup.

## Procedure-by-procedure

### 93015 — Treadmill stress
PA not required for bare treadmill. Nuclear stress (93015+93017
combined) requires PA via EviCore.

### 93306 — TTE
PA not required. Cigna applies a 6-month repeat-frequency rule.

### 93458 — LHC with contrast
**PA required (via EviCore).** Required documentation: prior non-
invasive study REPORT (not summary), CCS class or NYHA class as
appropriate, risk-stratification rationale tied to ACC/AHA AUC.
Turnaround: 4–7 business days. EviCore portal pends on any
checklist gap.

### 93620 — EP study
**PA required (via EviCore).** Documentation: arrhythmia with
mechanism, failed/contraindicated AAD trial, symptom severity.
Turnaround: 5–8 business days.

### 93224 — Holter
PA not required. Post-payment review on indication.
